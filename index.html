ABOUT THE ACADAMY

Welcome to Prathvi's Dance Academy! We believe that dance is a powerful form of expression and a beautiful journey of self-discovery. Our academy is dedicated to training students in various dance styles with precision, passion, and professionalism.




DANCE STYLE WE OFFER:

  1. Classical Dance: Bharatanatyam, Kathak
  2. Western Dance: Hip-Hop, Jazz, Contemporary
  3. Folk Dance: Garba, Bhangra, Lavani
  4. Bollywood Dance: Full of energy and expressions
  5. Freestyle: A creative blend of multiple styles for fun and fitness


We welcome students of all skill levels—from beginners to advanced dancers—and provide a nurturing environment to grow and shine on stage and in life.
